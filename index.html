Busy City - SmartBin

The Idea

SmartBin is an innovative project that aims to keep our cities cleaner and safer. It automatically sends SMS messages to the city council whenever a garbage bin is filled to its maximum capacity. If the methane gas level exceeds a certain threshold, an emergency message is also sent to alert the authorities.
